    Unit Mechanics

    Hybrid - A unit that can attack both fly/ground enemies, but can be placed on hill/ground

    Ground - A unit that can attack only ground enemies

    Hill - A unit that can attack both fly/ground enemies

    Enemy Mechanics

    Fly - Enemies that require either a Hybrid or Hill unit to hit the enemy.

    Speed - Enemies with an increased amount of speed.

    Tank - Enemies have more health.

    Status Effects

    Burn - 2.7x Unit damage over 4 ticks lasting 4 secs

    Freeze - 4 Seconds stop(doesnt stop with stun or timestop)

    Slow - Movement speed reduced by 33% lasting 10 seconds

    Bleed - 2.5x Damage over 5 ticks, lasting 6 seconds

    Stun - 2.5 Seconds stun(doesnt stack with frozen/timestop)

    Electrified - Stuns an enemy 3x for 1.5 seconds each and deal 2x units lasting 9 seconds

    Dismantled - Deals 4.7x damage over 6 ticks and Slows by 5% and lasting 9 seconds

    Crippled - 10% speed reduction(stacks with slow), 15% more damage taken

    Black Flames - Deals 14.25x of units damage across 20 ticks lasting 10 seconds

    Weakened - Deals 15% more damage for 10 seconds

    Terminologies

    Banner - Character Summoning, on the side panel you can see "summon" this will bring you to the banner.

    DPS - Damage Per Second, Relative to AOE, SPA, and Damage Output.

    CD - Cool Down, The waiting period of a manual ability of a unit.

    SPA - Seconds Per Attack, The wait time between each attack.

    AoE - Area of Effect, The style of attack, carrying with Single, Cone, Circle, or Full AoE.

    AoE(Cone) - A cone-shaped attack style, any enemies within the cone will be hit.

    AoE(Circle) - A circle-shaped attack style, enemies inside the circle will be hit.

    AoE(Full) - A AoE is taking the unit's entire range; anybody within the range will be hit.

    AoE(Line) - A AoE as a line, much like Cone AoE, no difference besides the end does not protrude.

    Meta - Shortened for MetaGame, Meta is usually the best strategy or route towards a goal.

    Inf - Shortened for Infinite, referring to the game mode.

    LB - An abbreviation for Leaderboard, where you will see the high scores on specific aspects of the game.
